ABOUT OUR MAHALLAH

In 1997, this Mahallah was founded. This Mahallah, which occupies around 6.5 acres, features 1 central facilities block and 7 residential blocks that can house up to 940 people. Sayyidina Abu Bakr as-Siddiq RA, his imposing demeanor, and his outstanding contributions to Islam and the Muslim Ummah are the reasons behind the Naming of this Mahallah. Like the other fifteen Mahallat on the IIUM Gombak campus, this one is jointly managed by the Student Affairs and Development Division and the Residential and Services Department.

Our Room

Welcome to our cozy Quad Room! Our Quad Room is designed to provide a comfortable and communal living space for up to four guests. Equipped with modern amenities, each room features comfortable beds, individual storage spaces, and a vibrant ambiance. Whether you're traveling with friends or making new ones, our Quad Room is the perfect blend of comfort and camaraderie.

Look Around

Immerse yourself in the breathtaking surroundings of our hostel! Our hostel offers a stunning backdrop of beautiful of Bukit Tabur. At our hostel, the scenic charm extends beyond the rooms, creating an unforgettable experience for our guests.

Our Office

Need assistance or have questions during your stay? Our hostel office is here to ensure your experience is seamless and enjoyable. Located at Level 2, Mahallah Siddiq Cafe, our dedicated staff is ready to assist you with check-in, provide local recommendations, and address any concerns you may have. Feel free to drop by anytime during our office hours, and let us take care of all your accommodation needs. Your comfort and satisfaction are our top priorities.
